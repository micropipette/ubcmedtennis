Who We Are

More Than Just Tennis

Building community and promoting wellness across UBC's medical program.

Est. 2022

UBC Medicine

140+ Active Members

Supporting Student Wellness

Medical school is demanding. We believe in the power of physical activity and social connection to support student wellbeing. Whether you've never held a racket or you're looking to maintain your competitive edge, there's a place for you here.

Our events range from casual rallies to structured tournaments, with opportunities to connect with students across all yearsincluding our distributed sites across British Columbia. Pickleball club is now part of UBC Med Tennis Club!
